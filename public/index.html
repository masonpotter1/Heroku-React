Welcome to NaturalAds

NaturalAds reshapes the boundaries between content and advertising. Our platform utilizes advanced Language Models (LLMs) to integrate advertisements seamlessly into chatbot conversations and website content, creating a non-intrusive, engaging, and contextually relevant advertising experience.

Who Uses NaturalAds?

  • Advertisers: Pay to have product and services mentioned organically in content and chatbot conversations.
  • Content Generation Sites: Newsites, blogs, and others monetize existing content. Existing articles are augmented on each user visit to mention products and services relevant to both the user and the article content.
  • Chatbot Sites: In the course of normal chatbot conversation, products and services are mentioned when the context and user desires are relevant.
  • Monetize Effortlessly: Offer websites and chatbot developers a new revenue stream without compromising on user experience and content quality.

Advertisers: Choose your target audience, define your budget, and create your advertising message.

Engage & Convert: Reach users organically as they interact with chatbots or browse websites, driving awareness and conversions.
